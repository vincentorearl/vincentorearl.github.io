My Work in Music, Media, and More

My experience in audio comes from my years of experience writing, producing, and engineering my own music, the audio production and music tech classes I have taken, and my work at festivals and in media. I have experience with both Digital Audio Workstations as well as live sound systems. At the 2024 New Orleans Jazz & Heritage Festival, I interned as a recording engineer for Munck Music, who operates 'Jazz Fest Live.' There, I was in charge of recording the stage audio from our rigs backstage, assuring clean audio quality, compiling setlists, and creating accurate input lists for our team (who then mix, and master, and distribute the performances as live albums).

In 2023, I completed an internship with WYES New Orleans, a non-profit, community broadcasting station affiliated with PBS. At WYES, I was a Digital Media intern, often editing to-be-aired programming as well as doing camera work for their Thursday local culture show, Steppin' Out with Peggy Laborde. For Steppin' Out, I shot live performances for musical guests, including Jason Scheff, bassist and vocalist for the legendary band Chicago, and Greg Barnhill, Emmy and and Grammy nominated songwriter and artist.

My experience in web design is seen in the two websites (including this one) that I made from scratch. I am currently learning Python, a more advanced language, and I am looking to start doing freelance web design soon. My first website, The 2005: Internet Magazine, was a digital take on the popular art magazine genre, where I showcased unique artists and wrote small blurbs about their artistc visions.
